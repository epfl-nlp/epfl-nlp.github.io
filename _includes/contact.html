  • I'm an EPFL Master's student

    Application

    Interested in doing a project with the NLP lab? First, select one or more projects you're interested in from this document, which contains detailed information about each project currently available (access requires your Tequila credentials).

    Then send an e-mail to:
    nlp-projects-apply@groupes.epfl.ch

    Please attach your CV and transcript and include [Masters Project] or [Masters Thesis] in your subject heading. Looking forward to collaborating with you!

    (Note: if you want to propose your own project (which should share at least some similarities with the projects currently on offer), please contact the supervisor first to see whether they would be open to it.)

  • I'm looking for a summer internship

    If you are a Bachelor's or Master's student at another university, please apply through the Summer@EPFL program. If you are looking for a PhD internship, contact me directly.

  • I'm applying to the EPFL EDIC PhD program

    I will be taking on new PhD students next year! Apply if you're interested in joining EPFL to work with me. Before you can be considered for the NLP lab, however, you will have to be admitted to the EDIC program, which handles admissions centrally. Feel free to let me know if you apply, but I unfortunately can't conduct pre-screenings until applications are in.

  • I'm an EDIC fellow

    I'm happy to supervise rotations provided our research interests align and there's a good chance that the rotation will lead to a permanent position in the lab.
